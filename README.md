# VCF to CSV/HTML Converter

A simple, fast, and privacy-focused tool to convert your VCF (vCard) contact files into CSV or HTML formats. 

## Features
- **Privacy-First:** All processing happens locally in your browser. Your data is never uploaded to any server.
- **Easy to use:** Select your VCF file, choose your preferred CSV separator, and download the results.
- **Versatile:** Exports to CSV (compatible with Excel/Google Sheets) and HTML (for easy viewing).

## How to use
1. Open the tool in your browser.
2. Select your `.vcf` file.
3. Choose the desired CSV separator (comma or semicolon).
4. Click "Convert Data".
5. Download your processed files.
